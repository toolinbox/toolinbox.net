url.html

Usage:

  • utm_medium, used for product.
  • utm_source, used for the operating channel.
  • url, the real url you want to jump. E.g., the Mac App Store link.
    • Note: The url should be encoded. Encode Online

Demo:

http://ex.toolinbox.net/ga/url.html?utm_medium=ihosts&utm_source=36kr&id=UA-12345678-1&url=https%3a%2f%2fitunes.apple.com%2fapp%2fid1056935452%3fls%3d1%26mt%3d12
